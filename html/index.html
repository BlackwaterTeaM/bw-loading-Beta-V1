City Ordinances

Adherence ensures a stable and immersive environment for all citizens.

Code of Conduct

1
Mutual Respect: Engage with civility. Harassment, discrimination, and toxic behavior are grounds for immediate expulsion.
2
Fair Play Protocol: Utilization of cheats, exploits, or unauthorized modifications undermines the city's integrity and incurs severe penalties.
3
Communication Clarity: A functional microphone is mandatory. Employ appropriate in-character and out-of-character communication channels.
4
Identity Integrity: Your digital identity (account) is your sole responsibility. Sharing access is prohibited.

Immersion Protocols

5
Character Persona: Maintain your established character identity consistently. Limit OOC chatter to essential communication via designated channels.
6
New Life Rule (NLR): Upon character incapacitation deemed "death," all memory preceding the event is lost. Avoid immediate return to the conflict zone or seeking retribution based on prior knowledge.
7
Valuation of Life (VDL): React realistically to threats and danger. Actions should reflect a reasonable desire for self-preservation.
8
Information Boundaries: Do not leverage OOC knowledge within IC scenarios (Metagaming). Do not dictate other players' actions or assume unrealistic capabilities (Powergaming).
9
Engagement Initiation: Random acts of violence without prior roleplay justification (RDM/VDM) disrupt the narrative and are strictly forbidden.
